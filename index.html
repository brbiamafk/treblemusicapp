Treble

Treble is a musician social network. We strive to connect musicians around the world, helping our community to collaborate, connect, and create beautiful music.

The Treble network beta will include the following offerings:

  • Jam Networking
  • Songwriting Collaboration
  • Sharing
  • Production and Mastering
  • Forums, Chatrooms and FAQs
  • Direct Chat and Video Conferencing
  • Profile Management and Reputation Builder
